Who is Aaron, an About Me

Hey there, this is my portfolio where you can see what projects I have worked on. I am a programmer based in Melbourne, but originally from South Africa. I moved to Australia in my pursuit of becoming a professional game programmer and am loving my new home. I enjoy bike rides on the weekends which has been a great way to explore the Melbourne area as well as get away from the computer. I'm quiet yet highly passionate and enthusiastic about my field and am always pushing myself to improve and expand my skills.

I primarily program in C++ and C# with a focus on game developement as well as other topics which interest me such as procedural generation and template meta programming (yikes). I am always working on a personal project exploring new ideas or consolidating newer skills.
